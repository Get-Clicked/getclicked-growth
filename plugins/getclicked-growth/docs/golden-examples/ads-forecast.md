## Breakeven Analysis

- **First-conversion breakeven:** CAC must be < $65. Requires ~7.7% CVR at $5.00 avg CPC — unlikely in month 1-2, possible by month 4-6 with landing page optimization.
- **LTV breakeven:** If avg patient books 2-3 times/year, LTV = $130-$195. At $77 CAC (optimized), every retained patient is profitable. Family Manager patients (whole household) are the highest-LTV segment.
- **Monthly breakeven at $1,000/mo:** Need $1,000 in revenue = ~16 visits = 6.2% CVR. Achievable by month 4-6 with optimized landing pages and conversion tracking.

## The Honest Take

First-visit ROAS is below 1.0x at every budget level and every scenario. This is normal for a new campaign with no conversion history. Here's why it's still worth it:

1. **You're buying data, not just clicks.** The first $1,000-$2,000 teaches you which keywords, ad groups, and audiences actually convert. That data makes everything after it cheaper.
2. **LTV makes the math work.** A Cash-Pay Patient or Family Manager who books once is likely to come back. One repeat visit doubles the revenue against the same acquisition cost.
3. **Documentation patients pay for themselves fastest.** "Doctors note online" keywords have the cheapest CPCs ($3-$7) and highest volume (9,900/mo). At 5% CVR, they hit $83 CAC — one repeat visit makes it profitable.
4. **Idaho is the testing ground.** Comp index of 6 means near-zero competition. Every dollar goes further there than in any other market.

---

## What Moves the Numbers

1. **Conversion rate is the biggest lever.** 3% to 5% doubles bookings without increasing spend. 5% to 7% gets you to first-visit breakeven.
2. **Negative keywords** drop avg CPC by eliminating wasted clicks. Run search term audit after 2 weeks.
3. **Landing page optimization** is the fastest CVR improvement — clear CTA above the fold, $65 price visible immediately, mobile-first, fast load.
4. **Ad group consolidation** after 4 weeks of data — pause underperformers, reallocate budget to winners.

## Recommendation

Start at **$1,000/month**. At optimized rates (month 3+), that's ~13 visits/month at $77 CAC. First-visit ROAS is 0.85x, but with even one repeat visit per patient it flips to 1.7x.

After 4-8 weeks of data, reallocate to whatever ad groups are converting and consider pushing to $1,500.
