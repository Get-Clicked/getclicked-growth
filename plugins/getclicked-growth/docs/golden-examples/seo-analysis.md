## Strategic Insights

1. **The "AI agent" category is Lindy's to own.** 49,500/mo search volume, MEDIUM competition, and nobody has a definitive page. The first company that builds the canonical "AI agent" content hub wins the category. Lindy should be to "AI agent" what HubSpot is to "inbound marketing."

2. **Integration pages are the single biggest growth lever.** Zapier's 57K ranked keywords come primarily from integration pages. Lindy has 3,000+ integrations with zero dedicated SEO pages. Even a basic `/integrations/slack` page with unique content beats the current logo grid.

3. **Blog strategy is strong but conversion-weak.** Lindy already ranks for 5,946 keywords via blog content (Otter.ai reviews, chatbot comparisons). But these posts drive traffic from people researching other tools — they need stronger Lindy CTAs and conversion pathways.

4. **Use-case landing pages are an easy win.** "AI email assistant" (2,400/mo), "AI meeting assistant" (3,600/mo), "AI scheduling assistant" (1,600/mo) — all LOW to MEDIUM competition. Nobody has a product page that owns these exact terms. Lindy can.
