## Strategy

One dedicated landing page per ad group. Each page has a single job: convert the visitor who just clicked a specific ad into a free trial signup or demo request. Message match is everything — the H1 on the page should feel like a direct continuation of the headline they clicked.

---

## Design Principles

- **One CTA per page.** Primary: "Start Free Trial" (above fold). Secondary: "Book a Demo" (below fold for enterprise buyers).
- **Message match.** The keyword they searched and the ad they clicked should be echoed in the page H1 and first paragraph.
- **Outcome > feature.** Lead with what changes for the user, then show how the product does it.
- **Social proof early.** "40,000+ professionals" or specific testimonial within first scroll.
- **No navigation distractions.** Minimal nav — logo, CTA button only. Remove full site navigation to reduce exits.
- **Mobile-first.** 70%+ of traffic will be mobile. Design for thumb zones and fast load.

---

## Page Template

Each page follows this structure:

1. **Hero** — H1 (keyword-matched), subhead (value prop), primary CTA, product screenshot/demo
2. **Social proof bar** — "Trusted by 40,000+ professionals" + logo strip or testimonial
3. **3 Benefits** — Outcome-focused cards mapping to the persona's pain points
4. **How it works** — 3 steps (Setup / Configure / Results)
5. **Testimonial** — Specific to the use case/persona if available
6. **FAQ** — 3-4 objection-handling questions
7. **Final CTA** — Repeat primary CTA + secondary "Book a Demo" link
