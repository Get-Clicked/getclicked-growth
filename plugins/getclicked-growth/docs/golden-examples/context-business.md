## Business

Tangerine Health Professionals PLLC operates at tangerinehealthpros.com as a telehealth virtual urgent care practice. The model is straightforward: cash-pay virtual visits with no insurance billing and no membership fees. Service area spans Washington, Idaho, and Colorado (multi-state licensed). Open 7 days a week with same-day appointments available. Every visit is $65, flat, regardless of service.

---

## Products and Services

- **Virtual Quick Care** — 15-minute telemedicine consultations for common urgent care needs (cold and flu, UTI, sore throat, ear infections, rashes, pink eye, allergies, minor strains)
- **Medication Refills** — non-controlled substance refills processed quickly (no narcotics or scheduled medications)
- **Medical Documentation** — sick notes, return-to-work clearances, lab and X-ray orders
- **Student/Employment Health** — school physicals, health clearances, chest X-rays, PPD readings, drug tests

The documentation and student/employment health services are the strategic wedge. National telehealth platforms don't touch this — it's genuine white space.

## Audience

**Primary:** People who need quick, affordable care without insurance hassles or long waits.

**Key Segments:**
- Tech-savvy adults without a primary care provider
- Students needing medical requirements (physicals, clearances)
- Workers needing employment health docs (X-rays, PPD, drug tests)
- People who need a prescriber but don't think "urgent care"
- Rural patients far from brick-and-mortar urgent care

**Demographics:** Skews younger/working-age, comfortable with telehealth, in smaller cities and rural areas across WA/ID/CO.

**Pain Points:** Can't get a same-day appointment, don't have insurance or have high deductibles, don't want to sit in a waiting room for a simple issue, need documentation fast.

**How They Search:** "online doctor," "telehealth urgent care," "virtual urgent care near me," "online UTI treatment," "$65 doctor visit," condition-specific queries.

## Value Proposition

Skip the waiting room, not the care you deserve. Same provider consistency, 15-minute virtual visits, $65 flat fee — no surprise bills, no insurance required. Accessible 7 days a week across three states.
